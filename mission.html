Our Mission

Driving Equality and Climate Action for a Sustainable Future

Our Mission

At the heart of our project lies a powerful mission: to model the relationship between climate change and gender inequality and to propose actionable solutions that promote both equality and sustainable development.

We aim to achieve this by focusing on two critical United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs):

  • Goal 5: Achieve gender equality and empower all women and girls.
  • Goal 13: Take urgent action to combat climate change and its impacts.

Our mission is based on several key principles:

  • Recognizing that women, especially in vulnerable communities, are disproportionately affected by climate change.
  • Empowering women and promoting gender equality as essential to effective climate action.
  • Building resilient communities by advocating for equal opportunities for women in climate leadership.

By bridging the gap between gender equality and climate action, we believe that we can create a more sustainable, inclusive future for all.
