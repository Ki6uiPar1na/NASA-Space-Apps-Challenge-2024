Climate Change & Gender Equality

A powerful relationship driving sustainable development.

Project Overview

The United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) outline targets for promoting human development and protecting the environment. Two critical goals focus on:

  • Goal 5: Achieving gender equality and empowering all women and girls.
  • Goal 13: Taking urgent action to combat climate change and its impacts.

Although gender equality and climate change might seem unrelated at first, they are intricately connected:

  • Women and girls are often disproportionately affected by the impacts of climate change, particularly in vulnerable communities.
  • Empowering women can lead to more effective and sustainable climate action.
  • Promoting gender equality helps build resilient communities that can better adapt to climate-related challenges.

Our project models this relationship and proposes solutions that promote both gender equality and climate action, driving sustainable development for all.
